Who I Am

My name is Abhishek Kumar and I’m a final semester master’s student in Computer Science at Texas Tech University. I currently work as a full-stack developer in Institutional Research (IRIM), Lubbock, Texas. I'm incredibly passionate about technologies, what I do, and I feel really excited towards pushing myself to the edge. Click here to see my latest work on Docker and Kubernetes.

I want to make things that make a difference. I work to the principle of keeping it simple, while carefully including the finest details and interactions.
As a design thinker, I’m interested in clean aesthetics, minimalism, typography and simplistic solutions.
As a developer, I try to write code that is easy to understand, easy to change and fun to work with. I strive for clean, elegant and efficient code to assure long-term sustainability and keep software's maintainability.

I also hold a master’s degree in Chemical Engineering from Texas Tech University where I worked as a research assistant in High Speed imaging and simulation of Fluids. My work was published in Physical Review, one of the top peer-reviewed journals. Learn more.
